How We Execute Deals

  • Owner Buyback

    We negotiate with note owners and provide them the opportunity to payoff their loans at a discount to what they owe.

  • Forlosure

    We proceed with traditional foreclosure procedures through every negotiation as a last resort to ensure that we continue the process.

  • Deed In Lieu

    We offer all property owners the option to relinquish the deed to their property in exchange for releasing them and their personal guarantee from any liability for the property's debt.

  • Refinance

    For properties with low debt LTV's relative to the properties market value, we work with the owners to help them refinance their deals and cover their payoffs.
